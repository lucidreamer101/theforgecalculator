Welcome to The Forge!

The Forge is a Roblox crafting game where you collect ores, combine them in a forge, and create powerful weapons and armor. Here's everything you need to know to get started.

ğŸ® Basic Mechanics

  • Mining: Explore the world and mine ore nodes. Different areas have different ores.
  • Forging: Combine up to 4 different ore types in the forge to craft items.
  • Multipliers: Each ore has a multiplier (0.2x to 11.5x) that affects your item's stats.
  • Probabilities: The total ores you add determines what items you can craft.

ğŸ“Š Understanding Multipliers

The average multiplier of your ore mix determines your weapon's damage or armor's defense. Higher is better!

  • Common ores (Stone, Copper): 0.2x - 0.5x multiplier
  • Uncommon ores (Gold, Silver): 0.5x - 1.3x multiplier
  • Rare ores (Diamond, Sapphire): 1.5x - 2.5x multiplier
  • Epic+ ores (Rivalite, Magmaite): 3x - 11.5x multiplier
